About WDYT

What Do You Think (WDYT) rewards the opinions of all Australians. It's free to join and once you become a member, in order to earn rewards and cash online,  simply maintain an active profile and complete surveys we send you. The more we know about you, the more you are likely to receive surveys... and the more surveys you complete, the more money you make… it's that easy.

1. Become a WDYT member

 Its free to become a WDYT member and takes about 2 mins to join.

2. Earn rewards for surveys

Once registered, you will receive surveys that fit your profile. Each time you complete a survey, you will accumulate rewards in your account.

3. Redeem rewards

Redeem rewards from a great range of partners OR choose to redeem your rewards for cash paid directly into your bank account. (View our partners here)

Our team

WDYT is owned by Researchify,  an Australian owned and managed market research business with offices in Sydney and Melbourne servicing clients all over Australia. Researchify is committed to providing a safe and secure platform for all Australians to have their say.

Privacy and Data Security: We take Privacy seriously, as such the WDYT platform encrypts the information you give us and our platform technology is ISO 27001:2013 certified. (ISO27001:2013 is an international standard for implementing, maintaining and improving an information security system).

You can review our comprehensive Privacy Policy here and website Terms of Service here. To become a member, you must read and accept both of these documents.
